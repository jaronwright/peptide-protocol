# Decision 005: Dosing Choices Per Subject

**Date:** March 2026
**Status:** Final

## BPC-157/TB-500: 500mcg/day (Both Subjects)

- Standard range is 250–500mcg/day
- 500mcg chosen as both subjects are active athletes (cycling, lifting, swimming) with higher tissue turnover
- 250mcg is more conservative/maintenance; 500mcg is more therapeutic
- At 500mcg, each 5mg vial lasts 10 days — clean and predictable

## KPV: 500mcg/day (Both Subjects)

- Standard range is 250–500mcg/day
- 500mcg chosen for stronger anti-inflammatory baseline to support multi-sport training load
- At 500mcg, each 10mg vial lasts 20 days

## GHK-Cu: 1mg/day (Subject A) vs 2mg/day (Subject B)

### Subject A — 1mg/day
- At 24, natural GHK-Cu levels are still near their peak (~200ng/ml)
- 1mg/day is the most commonly used research dose
- Sufficient for skin, hair, and tissue remodeling benefits
- Returns at 1mg are well-documented; going to 2mg isn't necessarily better for her age
- Only has 2 vials — at 1mg they last exactly one ON cycle

### Subject B — 2mg/day
- At 33, GHK-Cu levels have already declined ~25% from peak
- Higher dose restores more aggressively
- Connective tissue repair benefits are directly relevant (knee meniscus, QL/lower back)
- **Practical benefit:** At 2mg/day, each vial lasts 25 days — within the 28-day shelf life, meaning zero waste. At 1mg/day, the vial would last 50 days and 22mg would be wasted per vial.
- Has 8 vials, enough for multiple cycles at this dose

## Injection Route: SubQ for All

- All peptides administered subcutaneously (into fat layer under skin)
- Standard insulin syringes (U-100, 29-31ga, ½")
- Subject B may target BPC/TB4 injections near right knee or lower back for localized benefit
